# v0.42.58.2 migration: structured meeting fact quality

No database or configuration migration is required.

Structured Fireflies-style meeting summaries now use bounded long-form fact
extraction when they contain headings such as `Overview`, `Summary`,
`Action items`, `Key decisions`, or `Discussion`. Slack pages keep the chat
parser and machine-noise filtering behavior.

After upgrading and restarting workers, repeat the serial quality canary:

```bash
gbrain extract-conversation-facts --types meeting,slack --limit 20 --workers 1 --max-cost-usd 0.25 --yes
```

Inspect a sample of inserted facts and representative retrieval queries before
running a larger backfill. Updated structured meeting pages replace their
prior CLI facts on replay, so content edits do not accumulate duplicate facts.
